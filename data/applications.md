## Legend

**Status:**
- `Evaluated` — Reviewed by automation, awaiting your decision
- `Applied` — You submitted application
- `Contacted` — Company reached out
- `Interview` — In interview process
- `Offer` — Offer received
- `Rejected` — Company rejected
- `Declined` — You declined offer
- `SKIP` — Not interested, skip

**Interviewing:**
- Empty if none
- `Phone Screen` — Initial call
- `Technical` — Technical interview
- `Behavioral` — Behavioral/culture fit
- `Final` — Final round
- `Multiple` — Multiple ongoing

**Result:**
- Empty if ongoing
- `Offer` — Received offer
- `Rejected` — Rejected by company
- `Ghosted` — No response >30 days
- `Declined` — You declined
